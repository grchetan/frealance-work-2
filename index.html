Our Heritage

A Family Recipe Turned Into a Beloved Brand

What started in a small home kitchen in Indore in 1985 has grown into one of the most trusted names for authentic kachori across India. Founded by Shrimati Saraswati Devi Maheswari, our recipes have been passed down across three generations — every batch made with the same passion, the same spices, and the same love.

🏡
Home-Style CraftEvery kachori is made in small batches to preserve the original homemade texture and flavour.
🌶️
Heritage Spice BlendsOur masala is prepared using a 3-generation-old family formula — never shared, never compromised.
📦
Vacuum Packed for FreshnessState-of-the-art packaging ensures the kachori reaches you as fresh as the day it was made.
