Our Heritage

The Secret is in the Spices

For over three decades, Maheswari Kachori has been synonymous with the true essence of Indian street food. We started in a small traditional kitchen, roasting and grinding our own masalas to create the perfect balance of heat, tang, and crunch.

Today, we bring that exact same nostalgic flavor to your home, ensuring every kachori is stuffed with our signature lentil mix and fried to golden perfection.
